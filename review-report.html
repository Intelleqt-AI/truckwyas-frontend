Cross-tenant data leak — 6 dashboard views have no company filter
core/views.py · FleetOverviewView:401, DriverOverviewView:825, DriverPerformanceLeaderboardView:927, QuotesPipelineOverviewView:1000, DashboardOverviewView:2278, DashboardSignalsView:2321

These six APIView subclasses query Vehicle, Driver, Quote, Invoice, and Load without filtering by request.user.company. Any authenticated user on any tenant sees every other company's fleet, pipeline, and financial data.

Critical
Share token truncated to 8 chars — brute-forceable with sequential quote_id
core/views.py · send_to_customer:1758, PublicQuoteView:1775, PublicQuoteRespondView:1826

The full 256-bit secrets.token_urlsafe(32) is generated but only the first 8 characters are embedded in the share URL: quote.token[:8]. Validation uses quote.token.startswith(token) — so guessing 8 URL-safe chars (~47 bits) against a sequential integer quote_id is feasible with a small automated probe.

FixUse the full token in the URL. Replace quote.token[:8] → quote.token everywhere. Replace startswith comparison with hmac.compare_digest(quote.token, token) to prevent timing attacks.
